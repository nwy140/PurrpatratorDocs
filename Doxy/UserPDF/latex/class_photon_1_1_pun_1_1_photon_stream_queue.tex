\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \doxyref{Photon\+Stream\+Queue}{p.}{class_photon_1_1_pun_1_1_photon_stream_queue} helps you poll object states at higher frequencies than what \doxyref{Photon\+Network.\+Send\+Rate}{p.}{class_photon_1_1_pun_1_1_photon_network_a84b1a2edd7844e3f42b1e58e6b4fc9c8} dictates and then sends all those states at once when \doxyref{Serialize()}{p.}{class_photon_1_1_pun_1_1_photon_stream_queue_aa894acf4883faf5806f75f4d757022a4} is called. 

On the receiving end you can call \doxyref{Deserialize()}{p.}{class_photon_1_1_pun_1_1_photon_stream_queue_a576b08634a4833827b019071ddfefe09} and then the stream will roll out the received object states in the same order and time\+Step they were recorded in.
